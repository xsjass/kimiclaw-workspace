# Chapter 4: Social Media — Turning Followers Into Buyers

## The Platform Strategy

### TikTok (Priority #1)
**Why:** Algorithm favors new creators. Beat content goes viral daily.
**Content Types:**
1. **Type Beat Videos** — "Drake type beat" + trending sound
2. **Process Videos** — Making a beat from scratch (ASMR factor)
3. **Before/After** — Raw sample → finished beat
4. **Tutorial Snippets** — "How I made this 808 bounce"
5. **Behind the Scenes** — Studio setup, gear, workflow
**Posting Schedule:** 2-3x/day minimum
**Hashtags:** #beatsforsale #typebeats #musicproducer #trapbeats #beatmaker

### Instagram (Priority #2)
**Why:** Visual platform, good for brand building
**Content Types:**
1. **Carousel Posts** — 5-slide educational content
2. **Reels** — 15-30 sec beat previews
3. **Stories** — Daily studio updates, polls, Q&A
4. **IGTV/Long-form** — Beat breakdowns, tutorials
**Posting Schedule:** 1 carousel/day, 2-3 reels/week, stories daily

### YouTube (Priority #3)
**Why:** SEO goldmine. Long-form content builds authority.
**Content Types:**
1. **Beat Breakdowns** — "How I made [Beat Name]"
2. **Tutorials** — "How to mix 808s like a pro"
3. **Type Beat Compilations** — "10 Drake Type Beats"
4. **Vlogs** — Studio sessions, producer lifestyle
5. **Reviews** — Gear, plugins, platforms
**Posting Schedule:** 1-2 videos/week minimum

### Twitter/X (Priority #4)
**Why:** Networking. Producers, artists, industry people active here.
**Content Types:**
1. **Beat clips** — 30-second previews
2. **Hot takes** — "FL Studio is better than Ableton for trap" (engagement bait)
3. **Tips** — Quick production tricks
4. **Polls** — "Which beat should I drop today?"
5. **Threads** — Storytelling about your journey
**Posting Schedule:** 3-5x/day

## The Viral Formula
Every viral beat video has:
1. **Hook in first 1 second** — Beat drop or bold statement
2. **Visual movement** — Waveform, MIDI notes, studio lights
3. **Trending sound** — Use what's hot, add your beat
4. **Clear CTA** — "Link in bio" or "DM for exclusive"
5. **Consistent branding** — Same colors, fonts, style

## Engagement Tactics
- Reply to EVERY comment in first hour
- Duets/Stitches with trending beat videos
- Collaborate with other producers
- Go live: "Making a beat from your suggestions"
- Giveaways: "Free exclusive to whoever uses this best"

## Key Takeaway
Social media is your storefront. Every post is a sample. Every video is a commercial. Treat it like business, not hobby.
