# Chapter 6: SEO — Getting Found on Google

## Why SEO Matters for Producers
- "beats for sale" = 12,100 monthly searches
- "type beats" = 8,100 monthly searches
- "trap beats" = 6,600 monthly searches
- Organic traffic = free, sustainable, compounding

## Keyword Strategy

### Buyer Keywords (High Intent)
- "buy beats online"
- "exclusive beats for sale"
- "lease beats cheap"
- "trap beats download"
- "type beat [artist name]"

### Informational Keywords (Top of Funnel)
- "how to sell beats online"
- "beat licensing explained"
- "best platform to sell beats"
- "how to price beats"
- "beatstars vs airbit"

### Long-Tail Keywords (Easy Wins)
- "dark trap beats for sale"
- "afrobeats instrumental download"
- "phonk type beat 2026"
- "emotional piano beats"
- "drill beats UK"

## On-Page SEO for Beat Stores

### Title Tags
- Homepage: "Buy Beats Online | [Your Name] | [Genre] Type Beats"
- Beat Page: "[Beat Name] | [Genre] Type Beat | [BPM] BPM | Buy Now"
- Category: "[Genre] Beats for Sale | Lease & Exclusive | [Your Name]"

### Meta Descriptions
- Include target keyword in first 120 characters
- Add CTA: "Buy now" or "Listen free"
- Keep under 160 characters

### URL Structure
- Good: `/beats/trap-beat-140bpm`
- Bad: `/beat?id=12345`

### Image Alt Text
- "Dark trap beat artwork for [Beat Name]"
- "Producer [Name] studio setup"

## Content Marketing SEO

### Blog Topics That Rank
1. "How to Sell Beats Online in 2026" (informational)
2. "Beat Licensing Explained: Lease vs Exclusive" (informational)
3. "Top 10 Type Beat Producers to Watch" (listicle)
4. "How to Mix 808s Like a Pro" (tutorial)
5. "BeatStars vs Airbit: Which is Better?" (comparison)

### Content Update Strategy
- Update top-performing posts every 3 months
- Add new sections, stats, examples
- Refresh publication date
- Google rewards freshness

## Link Building

### Easy Wins
1. **Producer Directories** — Submit your store to beat producer lists
2. **Guest Posts** — Write for music blogs (reach out to 10/week)
3. **Social Profiles** — Link to store from all social bios
4. **Forums** — Help people on Reddit, include link in bio
5. **Collaborations** — Work with other producers, cross-link

### Anchor Text Strategy
- 50% branded: "[Your Name] beats"
- 30% generic: "buy beats here", "listen now"
- 20% exact match: "trap beats for sale"

## Technical SEO Checklist
- [ ] Mobile-friendly design
- [ ] Fast load time (< 3 seconds)
- [ ] SSL certificate (HTTPS)
- [ ] XML sitemap submitted to Google
- [ ] robots.txt configured
- [ ] Structured data (JSON-LD for products)
- [ ] Canonical tags (prevent duplicate content)

## Key Takeaway
SEO is the tortoise, not the hare. It takes 3-6 months to see results. But once it kicks in, it's free traffic forever. Start now.
