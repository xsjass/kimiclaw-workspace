# Chapter 1: The Foundation — Why Most Producers Stay Broke

## The Hard Truth
80% of music producers make less than $500/year from their beats.
Not because their beats are bad. Because they don't understand marketing.

## The Producer's Mindset Shift
**From:** "I make beats, people should find me."
**To:** "I build a brand, people seek me out."

## The Three Pillars of Beat Selling Success
1. **Product** — Beats that artists actually want
2. **Platform** — Where you sell (and why it matters)
3. **Promotion** — How you get found

## The $10K/month Math
- 200 leases/month at $30 = $6,000
- 20 premium licenses/month at $75 = $1,500
- 5 exclusives/month at $500 = $2,500
- **Total: $10,000/month**

It's not magic. It's math + execution.

## Your 90-Day Action Plan
- Days 1-30: Build catalog + choose platform + set pricing
- Days 31-60: Content creation + social media + email list
- Days 61-90: Scale winners + optimize + hire help

## Key Takeaway
You are not just a producer. You are a business owner who happens to make beats.
