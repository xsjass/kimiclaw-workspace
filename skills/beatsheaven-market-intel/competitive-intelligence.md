## Airbit (The Clean One)
- **Commission:** 0% on store sales, commission on marketplace sales
- **Pricing:** Free → $9.99/month
- **Strengths:** Clean UI, fast setup, analytics, mobile-friendly player, $45M+ paid to producers
- **Weaknesses:** Smaller community, fewer advanced features
- **Model:** Producer storefront focus (not marketplace discovery)

## Traktrain (The Curated One)
- **Commission:** 20% on sales
- **Pricing:** Free plan limited uploads → paid plans
- **Strengths:** Curated quality, underground/niche aesthetic, higher average sales
- **Weaknesses:** Smaller reach, niche-only, no negotiation features
- **Best for:** Experimental/underground producers

## Soundee (The Newcomer)
- **Commission:** 0% commission on ALL paid plans
- **Strengths:** Both bulk deal types (BeatStars only does BOGO, Airbit only does bulk pricing)
- **Weaknesses:** Newer, less brand recognition

## BeatPass (The Hybrid)
- **Model:** Subscription + exclusive sales
- **Commission:** 0% on commissions
- **Unique:** Recurring subscription payouts + exclusive deals

## Soundgine (The Control Freak)
- **Commission:** 0% on all plans
- **Strengths:** Full control, custom sites
- **Weaknesses:** DIY heavy
