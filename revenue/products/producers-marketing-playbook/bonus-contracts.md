# BONUS: Beat Licensing Contract Templates

## BASIC LEASE AGREEMENT

**BEAT:** [Beat Name]
**PRODUCER:** [Your Name]
**ARTIST:** [Buyer Name]
**DATE:** [Date]
**LICENSE FEE:** $[Amount]

**GRANTED RIGHTS:**
- Non-exclusive use of the beat
- Up to [2,500] unit sales (physical + digital)
- Streaming on all platforms
- No monetization rights (YouTube Content ID claims possible)
- No music video rights
- No synchronization rights (TV, film, games)

**PRODUCER RETAINS:**
- All ownership of the beat
- Right to lease to other artists
- Right to use in portfolio

**CREDIT:**
- Artist must credit: "Produced by [Your Name]"
- Credit must appear in song title, description, and metadata

**NO REFUNDS:**
- All sales are final on digital goods

**LICENSE FEE:** $[Amount]

**GRANTED RIGHTS:**

**PRODUCER RETAINS:**
- All ownership

## EXCLUSIVE LICENSE AGREEMENT

**BEAT:** [Beat Name]
**PRODUCER:** [Your Name]
**ARTIST:** [Buyer Name]
**DATE:** [Date]
**LICENSE FEE:** $[Amount]

**GRANTED RIGHTS:**
- Exclusive use (beat removed from store)
- Unlimited sales
- Full monetization rights
- Unlimited music videos
- Synchronization rights (TV, film, games, ads)
- Right to modify beat

**DELIVERABLES:**
- WAV file
- MP3 file
- Individual stems
- Trackout session file (if applicable)

**PRODUCER RETAINS:**
- Producer credit (must be credited)
- Portfolio rights (with artist permission)
- Cannot resell to anyone else

**SAMPLE CLEARANCE:**
- Producer warrants beat contains no uncleared samples
- If samples exist, producer is responsible for clearance
- Artist is not responsible for sample clearance
