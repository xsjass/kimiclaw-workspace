## PRO Plan Break-Even
Monthly fees ÷ Average profit per sale = Break-even sales

Example:
- PRO plan: $14.99/month
- Average sale: $30
- You keep: $30 (0% fees)
- vs Free plan: $27.30 (9% fees)
- Extra per sale: $2.70
- Break-even: $14.99 ÷ $2.70 = 5.6 sales

**You break even on the 6th sale every month.**
