# Chapter 7: Beat Licensing — Protect Your Work, Maximize Revenue

## Why Licensing Matters
- 60% of producers have never sold an exclusive
- 40% don't understand the difference between lease and exclusive
- **Result:** Lost revenue, legal disputes, burned bridges

## The Three Main Licenses

### 1. Basic Lease (Non-Exclusive)
**What the buyer gets:**
- MP3 file
- 1,000-2,500 unit sales limit
- Non-exclusive (beat stays in store)
- No monetization rights
- Cannot use in music videos

**What you keep:**
- All ownership
- Can sell same beat to unlimited artists
- Can use in your own projects

**Price:** $15-30

### 2. Premium Lease (Non-Exclusive)
**What the buyer gets:**
- WAV + MP3 + individual stems (trackouts)
- 5,000-10,000 unit sales limit
- Non-exclusive
- Monetization rights (YouTube, Spotify)
- Can use in music videos

**What you keep:**
- All ownership
- Can sell same beat to others (up to limit)
- Can use in your own projects

**Price:** $50-150

### 3. Exclusive License
**What the buyer gets:**
- WAV + MP3 + stems + trackouts
- Unlimited sales
- Full monetization rights
- Can use anywhere (videos, movies, games)
- Beat removed from your store

**What you keep:**
- Producer credit (must be credited)
- Cannot resell to anyone else
- Can use in your portfolio (with permission)

**Price:** $200-2,000+

## Contract Essentials

### Must-Include Clauses
1. **License Type** — Clearly state which tier
2. **Usage Rights** — What they can/can't do
3. **Sales Limits** — Exact number (if applicable)
4. **Term** — Perpetual or time-limited
5. **Credit Requirements** — How you must be credited
6. **Modification Rights** — Can they edit the beat?
7. **Sample Clearance** — Who's responsible?
8. **Refund Policy** — No refunds on digital goods

### Sample Clearance Warning
If your beat contains samples (songs, vocals, movie clips):
- YOU are responsible for clearing the sample
- The artist is NOT responsible
- Uncleared samples = lawsuits
- **Solution:** Use royalty-free samples only

## The Upsell Strategy

### Automatic Upsells
- After lease purchase: "Upgrade to Premium for $40 more"
- After premium purchase: "Make it exclusive for $200 more"
- Before checkout: "Bundle deal: 5 beats for price of 4"

### Email Upsells
- Day 7 after lease: "Your lease is selling well. Upgrade?"
- Day 30: "Artist hit their limit. Time to go exclusive?"
