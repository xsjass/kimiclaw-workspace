## What It Is

**Kimiclaw** is an autonomous 24/7 digital entrepreneurship skill. It's not just a prompt — it's a full **3-layer architecture** for making money online with zero human intervention:

- **Strategy Layer** — Decides WHAT to do (portfolio management, opportunity assessment, risk management)
- **Execution Layer** — Decides HOW to do it (web automation, API integration, content generation, service delivery)
- **Learning Layer** — Makes you smarter over time (performance analytics, market research, competitive analysis)
